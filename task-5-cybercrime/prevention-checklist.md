## Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist for College Students in India

1. Use strong and unique passwords for email, social media, banking, and academic accounts.
2. Enable two-factor authentication on important services, especially email and finance-related apps.
3. Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, CVV numbers, or banking passwords with anyone.
4. Verify website URLs carefully before entering login details, and avoid clicking unknown links.
5. Check the sender's email address before responding to urgent academic or financial messages.
6. Do not scan unknown QR codes, especially in money-related situations; receiving money does not require scanning a code.
7. Confirm the receiver name and UPI ID before approving any digital payment.
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, payments, or other sensitive logins.
9. Keep your phone, browser, and applications updated to reduce security risks.
10. Enable transaction alerts and set spending limits in banking and UPI applications where available.
11. Report suspicious fraud immediately through the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
12. In urgent cases, call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
