## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention

### Overview
This folder contains the work completed for **Task 5**, which focuses on understanding cybercrime and promoting safe online practices.

The objective of this task is to analyze a cybercrime scenario and create a **practical prevention guide** for students.

---

### Part A – Case Study

#### Topic: Cybercrime Awareness (e.g., Phishing / Online Fraud)

The case study explains:
- What the cybercrime is  
- How it typically happens (step-by-step)  
- Who is commonly targeted  
- Possible consequences (financial loss, data breach, identity theft)  

It highlights how attackers exploit:
- Urgency and fear  
- Fake links and emails  
- Lack of user awareness  

---

### Part B – Prevention Checklist

A **“Stay Safe Online” checklist** was created with actionable tips, including:

- Use strong, unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links  
- Verify unknown messages and emails  
- Use secure networks for transactions  
- Keep software and devices updated  

#### Financial Safety (UPI Specific)
- Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details  
- Verify payment requests before approving  

#### Reporting Channels
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline: **1930**

This task helps in:
